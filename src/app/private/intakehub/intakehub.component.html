COMPREHENSIVE ESTATE PLANNING INTAKE SYSTEM

Purpose: Set the tone, build trust, and understand the client's "why"

Opening Message/Video Topic:

  • Welcome and confidentiality assurance
  • Overview of the estate planning journey
  • "This is about love and legacy, not just legal documents"

Vision & Values Questions:

  • What prompted you to think about estate planning at this time?
  • What keeps you awake at night regarding your family's future?
  • When you imagine your life well-lived, what will stand out?
  • If you could look back from the end of your life, what would you want to have accomplished with your wealth?
  • What does "success" mean for your family?
  • What values do you want your legacy to reflect? (Options: self-reliance, education, philanthropy, family unity, entrepreneurship, service to others, etc.)
  • What's your biggest fear about what could happen to your family or assets if something happened to you?
  • What do you hope your family will say about you and remember most?
  • Are there any family stories or experiences that shape how you think about wealth and legacy?
